Introduction

About Me

I am an ANU student who majors in the Master of Computing and specialises in Machine Learning, a so-called girl in STEM. Although I am a science student and an engineer, I am not as tedious as the Stereotype. After all, I have many art and sports-related hobbies, such as dancing, singing and working out.

My Passion

I am passionate about combining what I know and learn with the things I enjoy. Fortunately, this assignment is an excellent chance for me to implement these thoughts.

As a dancer, I know that there are some obstacles for a dance beginner who would like to find more information about basic dance knowledge and some dance foundation tutorials, except attending high-cost dancing courses. Additionally, It is hard to explore more and expand their dance style and genres, even for experienced dancers, without proper guidance. Therefore, developing a free dancer's website, especially for dancers, is fantastic.

Mission Statement

Freely Dance is a free dancers' website offering essential dance knowledge and movement tutorials to dance-related persons interested in dancing, would like to explore dancing, and know more about dance genres, developments and crucial movements of various types of dancing.

Currently, on the market, the Majority of the analogous websites are commercial websites selling dance courses. So there are not too many websites sharing information and tutorials on dancing.

My Goals

  • Reduce the accessing time of obtaining dance information.

  • Lower the threshold for dance learning by freely access all the time.

  • Beautify the user interface, and attract more dancers.

  • Share the website broadly and increase its influence.

  • Increase ordinary viewers of the websites, and discover more people who are potentially interested in dancing.

  • Get suitable dancing tutorials and materials with the creators' permission.
